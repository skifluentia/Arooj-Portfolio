Video Types Produced

Type 1 · Cinematic Story
"The Wrong Text" — Professional Communication

2-minute cinematic story showing how one wrong message cost a professional their client. 10 scenes, consistent character, over-shoulder phone shots, emotional payoff. Teaches Business English lesson through story.

Type 2 · Urdu Emotional Reel
"Client Call Pe Kya Hua" — Urdu/Hindi Reel

18-second bilingual reel targeting Pakistani/Indian market. Woman in shalwar kameez loses client due to English hesitation — transforms through learning. Urdu text overlays, emotional acting direction.

Type 3 · Educational Short
Father & Child English Tip Series

12-second educational videos using father-child conversation in garden setting to teach one English phrase correction per video. High shareability format. Example: "Don't say sorry — say thank you for your patience."

Type 4 · Brand Ad
30-Second Bilingual Advertisement

Structured 30-second ad — hook, problem, solution, credentials, CTA. Urdu voiceover with English course names. Designed for Meta Ads with honest claims only (no fabricated student numbers).
